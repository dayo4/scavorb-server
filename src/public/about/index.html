Do you build Single Page web Applications(SPAs)
Adedayo Adeniyi

Yes! SPAs are web apps that give you a similar mobile apps experience on the web without the need to download and install on your device. Progressive, faster navigation, fully customized, and no loading between pages...

A good example is scavorb.com created from scratch by me, both frontend and backend in Javascript technologies.

Unlike most websites built on wordpress requiring mostly basic templates to setup, SPAs are most often custom built for your specific purpose and will have better overall user experience, without requiring too much more resources.
