So you build Single Page web Applications(SPAs)
Adedayo Adeniyi

Yes! SPAs are web apps that give you a similar mobile apps experience on the web without the need to download and install on your device. Progressive, faster navigation, fully customized, and no boring loadings between pages...

A good example is scavorb.com custom built by me, both frontend and backend in Javascript technologies.

Unlike most traditional websites setup on wordpress and the likes, SPAs are most often more custom built for your specific purpose and will have better overall user experience, without requiring too much more resources.

Likewise i create wordpress websites if it's what you need, as there are ways to still take advantage of it's CMS functionalities in SPAs. Whether personal or ecommerce websites.
